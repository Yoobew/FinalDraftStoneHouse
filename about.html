Stonehouse Warrandyte

STONEHOUSE WARRANDYTE is an iconic landmark located in Warrandyte which was built in 1939. Fast-forward to today, and this historic building has transformed into a spacious café and restaurant serving modern-Australian cuisine, championing local produce. STONEHOUSE WARRANDYTE is helmed by husband and wife duo and locals, Deon and Stephanie Batticciotto and long-time friend Lee Papoulis since 2020. Spanning over 20 years of hospitality experience, the trio bring a wealth of knowledge having owned and operated multiple cafes and restaurants throughout Melbourne’s CBD as well resumed roles as head Chef in many more. STONEHOUSE WARRANDYTE is a place where everything’s seriously good, but not too serious, with all the familiarity of old-town nostalgia and the comfort of your favourite food.
